# Launch Kit

## Public positioning

Jungle Game should present as a serious cinematic survival project in active development, even before the first playable build is public.

## Repo bio

Cinematic Windows PC jungle survival project focused on realistic terrain, psychological pacing, and premium atmosphere.

## Short pitch

A cinematic jungle survival game for Windows PC where beauty, danger, and curiosity constantly pull the player deeper into the rainforest.

## Social post template

Working on Jungle Game, a cinematic rainforest survival project for Windows PC.

The goal is simple: make the jungle feel beautiful enough to lure you forward and dangerous enough to make every step matter.

#JungleGame #IndieGame #GameDev #SurvivalGame

## Trailer structure

### Shot 1

- Slow jungle canopy reveal
- Ambient wildlife and distant water

### Shot 2

- Rain and fog sweeping across the path
- Brief glimpse of movement in foliage

### Shot 3

- Player crosses a narrow ledge or river
- Audio tightens

### Shot 4

- Sudden threat cue
- Hard cut to black or title

### Shot 5

- Ruin reveal or waterfall vista
- Logo and repo/site URL
