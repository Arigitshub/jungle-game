# Brand and SEO Brief

## Positioning

Jungle Game should read as a serious cinematic survival project, not a throwaway asset-flip concept. The language needs to signal realism, atmosphere, and premium intent.

## Core phrases

- cinematic jungle survival game
- realistic jungle game for Windows PC
- atmospheric rainforest adventure
- psychological survival exploration game
- indie jungle survival project

## Metadata rules

- Lead with "Jungle Game" in titles
- Pair brand with "Windows PC" and "cinematic jungle survival"
- Keep descriptions under 160 characters when possible
- Use strong image-forward copy for social snippets

## Social content angles

- Beauty versus danger
- Rainforest realism
- Before-and-after prototype progress
- Landmark screenshots
- Sound design and atmosphere clips

## Hashtags

- #JungleGame
- #IndieGame
- #GameDev
- #SurvivalGame
- #IndieDev
- #CinematicGame
- #UnrealEngine or #Unity3D once engine is chosen

## Viral packaging guidance

- Every public post needs one striking visual hook.
- Show scale: cliffs, waterfalls, canopy depth, ruins.
- Avoid generic devlog wording.
- Use short, assertive copy that sells the feeling first and the feature second.

## Store and platform assets to prepare

- Capsule art
- Hero banner
- Social card image
- Logo lockup
- 10 to 20 second teaser clips
