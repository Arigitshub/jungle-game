# Vertical Slice Plan

## Goal

Ship a short, polished prototype that proves the emotional loop of Jungle Game.

## Target runtime

10 to 15 minutes for a first external playtest.

## Sequence

### 1. Calm entry

- Player enters a humid jungle path
- Low wind, bird calls, visible sunlight breaks
- First resource pickup and traversal tutorial

### 2. Environmental pressure

- Rain starts
- Visibility drops
- Route becomes harder to read
- Sounds imply a nearby threat without showing it

### 3. First danger

- Player crosses a narrow riverbank or cliff route
- Predator cue or near encounter triggers stealth movement
- Recovery space follows immediately after

### 4. Beauty beat

- The jungle opens into a waterfall vista or overlook
- Sound widens
- Player gets a strong emotional contrast after danger

### 5. Discovery beat

- Hidden ruin or cave chamber
- Visual reward plus story fragment
- End on a strong screenshot-worthy scene

## Must-build list

- Playable terrain chunk
- Traversal controller
- Rain and fog transition
- Basic stealth/detection logic
- Landmark vista
- Ruin reveal sequence

## Nice-to-have list

- Inventory panel
- Collectible relic
- Basic save/checkpoint
- Dynamic soundtrack stem changes

## Cut list if schedule slips

- Complex crafting
- Multiple enemy types
- Dialogue system
- Combat depth beyond escape and evasion
